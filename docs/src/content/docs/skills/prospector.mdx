> MicroSaaS opportunity discovery engine. Scans communities (Reddit, HN, IndieHackers, Twitter/X), market data (Google Trends, app stores, G2), technical signals (GitHub trending, npm/PyPI, Stack Overflow), SEO gaps, and competitor intel to surface bootstrappable microSaaS ideas. Triages on 6 dimensions, tracks lifecycle from discovery to launch, and manages a web dashboard.

## How It Works

<Steps>

1. **Wave 0 — Triage** (inline)

   Checks for interrupted sessions, runs the signal scanner for pre-analysis, probes available search tools, and presents a plan for user approval before executing.

2. **Wave 1 — Signal Discovery** (parallel subagents)

   One subagent per source category (community, market, technical, SEO, competitor). Each subagent searches for signals, then deep-reads the top URLs for full-text analysis.

3. **Wave 2 — Opportunity Crystallization** (inline)

   Clusters related signals into 8-15 candidate opportunities. Assigns qualitative triage ratings on 6 dimensions. Validates with deterministic `triage-validator.py`.

4. **Wave 3 — Validation** (parallel subagents)

   For each top candidate: searches for existing competitors, counter-evidence, demand freshness, and source independence. Re-runs triage validation for final tier.

5. **Wave 4 — Synthesis** (inline)

   Ranks by tier and signal count, produces opportunity cards with evidence and counter-evidence, saves to SQLite, and recommends expert panel evaluation for Strong-tier opportunities.

</Steps>

## Triage System

Every opportunity is scored on 6 dimensions, then assigned a tier by the deterministic `triage-validator.py` script:

| Dimension | Weight | What "strong" means |
|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Bootstrappability | High | Solo-dev MVP in less than 2 weeks, under $100/mo infra |
| PMF Signals | High | 2+ independent pain signals, payment evidence present |
| Competition | Medium | No direct competitor OR only weak/expensive ones |
| Revenue Potential | Medium | Realistic $5K+ MRR ceiling, pricing power exists |
| Technical Feasibility | Medium | Standard web stack, well-documented APIs |
| Moat Potential | Low | Data/network effects, integration lock-in possible |

**Tier assignment:**
- **Strong:** 4+ dimensions strong with 0 weak, or 3 strong including PMF Signals with 0-1 weak
- **Moderate:** 2-3 dimensions strong, 2 or fewer weak, no critical weakness in PMF + Bootstrappability
- **Weak:** 1 or fewer strong, or PMF Signals is weak, or Bootstrappability is weak

<Aside type="caution" title="Hard rules">
  Single-signal opportunities are capped at Moderate. Signals older than 6 months cap at Weak. A strong incumbent forces Competition to weak. These rules are enforced deterministically by `triage-validator.py` and cannot be overridden.
</Aside>

## Critical Rules

1. Never fabricate evidence — every quote traces to a real URL
2. Single-signal opportunities cannot reach Strong tier
3. Deep-read is mandatory when tools are available; degraded mode caps at Moderate
4. Always present Wave 0 triage to user for approval before executing
5. 5-10 opportunities per session maximum
6. Never skip Wave 3 (validation) — counter-evidence search is mandatory
7. Every opportunity card must include "Why This Might Be Wrong"
8. Database writes after every wave (crash recovery)
9. Max tool calls per session: ~80 total
